Features

Everything You Need to Prevent Cheating

Six layers of AI-driven detection working simultaneously to ensure every exam is conducted with complete integrity.

👤

Face Detection

Detects if a student is present, absent, or if multiple people are in frame — all in real time using Haar Cascade models.

👁️

Eye Tracking

MediaPipe Face Mesh tracks gaze direction. Looking left, right, or away from the screen triggers an immediate alert.

🧭

Head Pose Estimation

3D head orientation is computed every frame. Suspicious head turns away from the camera are flagged automatically.

📱

Object Detection

YOLOv8 scans the scene for prohibited items such as mobile phones, books, and secondary devices.

🖥️

Tab Switch Detection

Browser visibility and focus events are monitored. Repeated tab switches escalate warnings and can terminate the session.

📊

Admin Dashboard

Administrators get a live overview of all sessions, cheating logs with timestamps, and evidence snapshots for review.
